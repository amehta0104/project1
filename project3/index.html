POST MALONE

About

Blending the worlds of alternative and bass music, Strocksu is an embodiment of what it means to be human. Stephen Strocksu brings new meaning to storytelling by creating a world meant to show the power of being one’s self through powerful drops and even more powerful breakdowns, both of which evoke a sense of belonging in whatever world the listener dwells. With a heavy focus on melody and flow, Strocksu is overcoming the limitations of what it means to be a bass artist in the 21st century by breaking out of the conventional box of sound design and club sounds, instead bringing a more tasteful and relatable message of finding peace through one’s own mistakes and shortcomings.
