Post Malone, Tame Impala, and others to Headline Hangout Music Festival

Hangout Music Fest has announced the lineup for its highly-anticipated return to the beaches of Gulf Shores, Alabama from May 20-22. Post Malone, Phoebe Bridgers, Tame Impala, Megan Thee Stallion, Halsey, and more are set to headline. For three days, attendees will gather together on the white sand beaches of Southern Alabama’s famed Gulf Shores for a community-focused festival that has become a favorite for fans and performers alike since its debut in 2010. Part music festival and part immersive travel experience, guests will also get the chance to take part in a variety of activities and events that help to make Hangout a true music vacation. Additional headliners and highlights on the 2022 Hangout lineup include R&B and rap sensation Doja Cat, iconic Chicago pop-punk group Fall Out Boy, beloved dance music stand out ILLENIUM, award-winning country vocalist Kane Brown, Louisville rapper Jack Harlow, Grammy-winning German-Russian DJ and producer Zedd, Texas-born country-pop artist Maren Morris, soul singer and songwriter Leon Bridges, and more.
